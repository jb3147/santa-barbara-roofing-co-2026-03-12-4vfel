Our Services

Professional roofing services for Santa Barbara homes and businesses

Santa Barbara Roofing Co delivers expert installation, licensed workmanship, and transparent, free estimates for every project—from minor repairs to full roof replacements.

Roofing solutions built for coastal conditions

Every roof we work on is designed to handle Santa Barbara’s sun, salt air, and seasonal storms—installed by fully licensed contractors who treat your property like their own.

Residential Roof Repair

We locate and fix leaks, storm damage, and aging materials before they spread, using quality components that blend seamlessly with your existing roof. Our crews work efficiently and leave your property spotless.

Commercial Roofing Services

From flat roofs to multi-unit buildings, we install and maintain durable commercial roofing systems that minimize downtime. Every project is completed to code by licensed contractors with clear communication at each phase.

New Roof Installation

We install complete roofing systems engineered for coastal exposure, combining high-performance underlayments, flashing, and finishes. You’ll receive a clear scope, timeline, and warranty options before any work begins.

24/7 Emergency Roofing

When sudden leaks or storm damage threaten your home or business, our emergency team responds around the clock. We secure the structure, prevent further water intrusion, and provide a straightforward repair or replacement plan.

Roof Inspections & Maintenance

Routine inspections catch small issues before they turn into costly damage. We assess flashing, sealants, ventilation, and surfaces, then provide a prioritized maintenance plan with photos and clear recommendations.

Roof Coatings & Weatherproofing

Extend the life of your roof with reflective coatings and sealants designed for Santa Barbara’s sun and marine layer. Our licensed crews apply systems that improve energy efficiency and add long-term protection.
